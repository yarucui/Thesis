\section{Mixed-effects regression coefficients}
\label{app:regression-table}

\Cref{tab:m_mixedlm_coefs} reports the fixed-effect coefficients, 95\%
confidence intervals, and $p$-values from the mixed-effects regression
defined in \cref{eq:m_mixedlm}. The model was estimated by restricted
maximum likelihood on the 620-post advisor baseline (2{,}472 qualified
post--model cells). Reference categories omitted from the table are:
\texttt{reversibility} = \texttt{irreversible}, \texttt{time\_horizon}
= \texttt{long}, \texttt{resource\_constraint} = \texttt{financial},
\texttt{ses\_level} = \texttt{high}, \texttt{domain} = \texttt{career}.

\begin{table}[h]

  \label{tab:m_mixedlm_coefs}
  \begin{tabular}{lrlc}
